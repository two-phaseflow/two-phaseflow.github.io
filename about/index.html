I am a scientist who recently completed a PhD and period of post-doctoral research in the Fluids and Mineral Resources group of the Institute of Gechemistry and Petrology at ETH Zurich in Zurich, Switzerland. I'm interested in how the hydrology and chemistry of fluids in high-temperature geothermal systems can improve our ability to produce clean, reliable renewable energy from these systems. I'm currently living in Copenhagen, Denmark with my girlfriend Beeke Stegmann, and am keeping my eyes open for the right employment opportunity in the geothermal industry.
